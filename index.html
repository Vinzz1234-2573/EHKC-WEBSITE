What We Offer

Our Elder Support
Service Pillars

A comprehensive range of care services, each thoughtfully designed to meet the unique needs of every senior citizen we are privileged to serve.

Financial Assistance

We provide meaningful financial support to elderly individuals facing hardship, covering essential medical expenses and daily living costs so they can live with security, stability, and peace of mind throughout their golden years.

Learn more

Elderly Home Placement

We offer a compassionate and loving space in our elderly home for seniors in need — a safe and supportive environment complete with full-time care, dedicated staff, and comprehensive medical assistance to ensure their complete wellbeing.

Learn more

Collaborative Support

We actively collaborate with partner elderly homes to expand our care network, providing mutual support to seniors across communities throughout Malaysia — because no elder should ever be without help or companionship.

The Heart of Amitabha

In Compassion,
We Change The World

Founded in 2000, Amitabha Foundation Elder Home (Old Klang Road) has been providing four daily meals, 24/7 round-the-clock care, and spiritual support to seniors — so that every elder lives their golden years with dignity. Donations are welcome and are tax-deductible.

阿弥陀佛关怀老人院（旧巴生路）于2000年成立，提供四餐、全天照护与心灵支持，让长者安享晚年。接受捐款，并可申请税务减免。

  • Est. 2000 — Over 20 years of dedicated elder care on Old Klang Road, Kuala Lumpur.
  • 4 Meals Daily — Nutritious meals served four times a day to every resident in our care.
  • 24/7 Care & Spiritual Support — Full-time staff and compassionate spiritual guidance throughout.
  • Tax-Deductible Donations — Your contributions are welcome and qualify for tax exemption.
How It Works

Getting Help Is Simple

We have made it as easy as possible for families and seniors to access the care and support they deserve.

01

Reach Out

Call our hotline, WhatsApp us, or fill in the contact form. We are always ready to listen with compassion.

02

Needs Assessment

Our team meets with you to understand the specific needs and situation of your loved one in detail.

03

Care Plan

We develop a personalised support plan — financial aid, home placement, or collaborative network care.

04

Ongoing Support

We remain by your side with continuous check-ins, adjustments, and unwavering compassionate care.
